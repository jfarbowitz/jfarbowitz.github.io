Recent Projects

As part of my fellowship at the Guggenheim Museum, I supported the restoration of the web artwork Brandon, commissioned by the museum in 1998. Read a blog post I co-authored about the restoration process.

My thesis project involved an in-depth study of malware preservation. The full text of the thesis is currently available through the Moving Image Archiving and Preservation program's digital archive. You can also read a short article that summarizes my thesis research on The Conversation.

I was previously involved in the development of vrecord, open source software for archival videotape digitization. Check it out.
