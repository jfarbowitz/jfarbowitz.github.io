Jonathan Farbowitz is a conservator of time-based media art

I currently work as the Associate Conservator of Time-Based Media at the Metropolitan Musem of Art. Previously I was the Guggenheim Museum's Fellow in the Conservation of Computer-Based Art. I've written about preserving malware in archives, musuems, and libraries and helped develop open-source software for the audiovisual preservation community. Read more about me.
