Jonathan Farbowitz: conservator of time-based media art

I currently work as the Associate Conservator of Time-Based Media at the Metropolitan Musem of Art. Previously I was a Fellow in the Conservation of Computer-Based Art at the Solomon R. Guggenheim Museum. I've written about preserving malware in archives, musuems, and libraries and helped develop open-source software for the audiovisual preservation community. Read more about me.
